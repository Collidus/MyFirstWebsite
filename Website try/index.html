How Connect Works

Here is a short summary on how our app Connect works:

Register

Users can register for free and start using our platform without any form of fees taken.

Interact

Users on our platform have many ways to interact, whether it be posts or direct messages.

Encryption

Before being transmitted, the data (Posts or messages) are encrypted to get an additional layer of security. Every form of data has it's own unique encryptiom.

Boosting

To make data transmission efficient, the data is boosted and optimised to get maximum efficiency.

Transmission

When data is encrypted and boosted, the data is transmitted through Data Nodes to the recipient.

Reception

The data is received by the recipient's Connect client, then it's decrypted. Thereafter recipient can read the data.
